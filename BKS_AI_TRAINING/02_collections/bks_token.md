# BKS Token — Collection Brief

## DNA

Digital artifacts. Cryptographic imagination. Visual objects from the digital layer.

BKS Token explores the visual language of blockchain, digital ownership, and cryptographic systems — reinterpreted as AI-generated all-over print surfaces. Not crypto-branded, but crypto-sourced visually.

## Mood

Digital. Cool. Precise. Algorithmic. Object-like.

## Palette

- Dominant: dark green, near-black, deep teal
- Secondary: cool grey, muted gold, silver
- Accent: digital amber, pale cyan

## Pattern DNA

Grid-based forms. Hash-like pattern structures. Layered digital artifacts. QR-adjacent geometry. Object-like precision. Cold, algorithmic beauty.

## Product types in this collection

- Backpacks
- Tees
- Hoodies
- Windbreakers
- Puffer jackets
- Caps (if available)

## Naming convention

`BKS Token [Product Type] — [Variant]`

Examples:
- BKS Token Backpack — Signal
- BKS Token Tee — Hash
- BKS Token Hoodie — Block
- BKS Token Windbreaker — Chain

## Target customer

Tech-aware, digital culture, design-sophisticated, 22–40, drawn to digital aesthetics without literal crypto branding.

## Photography direction

Cold studio or industrial. LED light. Dark backgrounds. Minimal props. Clean, object-focused.
