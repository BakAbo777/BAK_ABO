# BKS Flag — Collection Brief

## DNA

Manifesto. Graphic identity. Visual fields as statements.

BKS Flag is the most declarative collection in the BKS system. Patterns function as graphic statements — bold fields, strong contrasts, identity-as-surface. The most instantly recognizable visual language.

## Mood

Bold. Declarative. Graphic. Identity-forward. Strong contrast.

## Palette

- Dominant: bold black + white fields, strong primary contrasts
- Secondary: deep red, navy blue (used as graphic fields, not decoration)
- Accent: pure white line, signal mark

## Pattern DNA

Flag-inspired geometry. Graphic field division. Bold contrast structures. Identity-mark surfaces. Declarative visual objects.

## Product types in this collection

- Tees
- Windbreakers
- Athletic shorts
- One-piece swimsuits
- Hoodies
- Puffer jackets

## Naming convention

`BKS Flag [Product Type] — [Variant]`

Examples:
- BKS Flag Windbreaker — Manifesto
- BKS Flag Tee — Field
- BKS Flag Athletic Shorts — Burst
- BKS Flag One-Piece Swimsuit — Drop

## Target customer

Strong visual identity preference, graphic-bold aesthetic, 20–40, confident dresser.

## Photography direction

Strong directional light. High contrast. Outdoor or raw industrial. Bold, clean backgrounds. Garment as the statement.
