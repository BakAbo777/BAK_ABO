# BKS Hours — Collection Brief

## DNA

Time. Measure. Urban silence. Geometric temporality.

BKS Hours is the most minimal and conceptual collection in the BKS system. It explores the language of time through abstract geometries, repeating patterns, and quiet visual rhythms.

## Mood

Measured. Silent. Architectural. Monochromatic. Urban.

## Palette

- Dominant: charcoal, warm black, deep navy
- Secondary: ivory, warm grey, cement
- Accent: graphite lines, cool white, pale sand

## Pattern DNA

Geometric repetition. Grid structures. Clock-like divisions. Abstract time markers. Thin lines. Negative space. Structured silence.

## Product types in this collection

- Puffer jackets
- Lounge pants
- Tees
- Sweatshirts
- Hoodies
- Athletic shorts
- Leggings

## Naming convention

`BKS Hours [Product Type] — [Variant]`

Examples:
- BKS Hours Puffer Jacket — Ink
- BKS Hours Lounge Pants — Pane
- BKS Hours Tee — Grid
- BKS Hours Sweatshirt — Silence

## Target customer

Urban professional, conceptual fashion lover, minimal aesthetic, 25–45, values quiet design over loud branding.

## Photography direction

Brutalist architecture. Concrete interiors. Long shadows. Late afternoon light. Minimal color. Black or ivory backgrounds.
