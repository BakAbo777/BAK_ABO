# BKS Glyph — Collection Brief

## DNA

Signs. Symbols. Future writing. Ancient graphic language.

BKS Glyph explores the boundary between historical mark-making and AI-generated symbolic surfaces. Patterns feel like a visual language — partly runic, partly futuristic, wholly graphic.

## Mood

Symbolic. Intellectual. Graphic. Layered. Bold but controlled.

## Palette

- Dominant: ivory, warm white, deep black
- Secondary: muted blue, steel grey, graphite
- Accent: controlled lilac, pale gold lines

## Pattern DNA

Symbolic marks. Glyphic repetition. Abstract writing systems. Dense symbol fields. Strong contrast between light ground and dark marks.

## Product types in this collection

- Sneakers
- Puffer jackets
- Tees
- Sweatshirts
- Backpacks
- Leggings
- Athletic shorts

## Naming convention

`BKS Glyph [Product Type] — [Variant]`

Examples:
- BKS Glyph Sneakers — Shade
- BKS Glyph Puffer Jacket — Cross
- BKS Glyph Backpack — Script
- BKS Glyph Tee — Mark

## Target customer

Graphic-culture enthusiast, typography lover, design-aware, 20–38.

## Photography direction

Urban architectural surfaces. Exposed concrete. Old walls with visible texture. Strong directional light creating hard shadows. Black and white leaning.
