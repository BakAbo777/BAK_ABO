# BKS Origin — Collection Brief

## DNA

Roots. Organic forms. First grammar. The founding visual language of BKS.

BKS Origin is where the BKS system began. It carries the original visual vocabulary: organic shapes, earthy materials references, and the earliest AI-generated pattern language of the brand.

## Mood

Organic. Warm. Foundational. Textured. Raw.

## Palette

- Dominant: earth tones, sand, warm ochre, olive
- Secondary: burnt sienna, terracotta, raw linen
- Accent: deep forest green, warm black

## Pattern DNA

Organic textures. Irregular forms. Nature-derived geometry. Cellular patterns. Layered AI-generated surfaces. Warm earth surfaces.

## Product types in this collection

- Tees
- Sweatshirts
- Hoodies
- Leggings
- Puffer jackets
- Lounge pants

## Naming convention

`BKS Origin [Product Type] — [Variant]`

Examples:
- BKS Origin Tee — Root
- BKS Origin Hoodie — Earth
- BKS Origin Sweatshirt — Clay

## Target customer

Earthy aesthetic, nature-conscious design lover, raw material textures, 22–40.

## Photography direction

Natural surfaces. Stone, sand, raw concrete. Outdoor or raw indoor. Warm golden light. Minimal staging.
