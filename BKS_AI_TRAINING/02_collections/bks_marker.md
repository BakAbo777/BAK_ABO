# BKS Marker — Collection Brief

## DNA

Urban traces. Street grammar. Gestural codes. The city as surface.

BKS Marker translates the visual language of urban environments — tags, stencils, signage, directional codes — into structured AI-generated all-over print patterns.

## Mood

Urban. Direct. Gestural. Street-informed. Energetic but not aggressive.

## Palette

- Dominant: black, white, cement grey
- Secondary: warm asphalt, off-white, cool stone
- Accent: spray-can orange, signal yellow (controlled)

## Pattern DNA

Gestural marks. Urban coding. Stencil-like forms. Directional lines. Grid overlays. Street-sourced visual logic.

## Product types in this collection

- Athletic shorts
- Tees
- Windbreakers
- Hoodies
- Leggings
- Backpacks

## Naming convention

`BKS Marker [Product Type] — [Variant]`

Examples:
- BKS Marker Athletic Shorts — City
- BKS Marker Tee — Code
- BKS Marker Windbreaker — Signal
- BKS Marker Hoodie — Trace

## Target customer

Urban active wear, street-culture adjacent, movement-oriented, 18–35.

## Photography direction

Street environments. Asphalt, walls, urban furniture. Dynamic poses. Natural daylight. Cities — not studios.
