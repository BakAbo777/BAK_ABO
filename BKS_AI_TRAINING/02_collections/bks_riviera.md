# BKS Riviera — Collection Brief

## DNA

Mediterranean light. Coast. Speed. Architectural summer.

BKS Riviera captures the energy of the Italian and French coastline through AI-generated surfaces that feel warm, bright, and kinetic — without becoming clichéd beach fashion.

## Mood

Bright. Light-filled. Mediterranean. Architectural. Warm energy.

## Palette

- Dominant: azure blue, warm sand, terracotta
- Secondary: coral, dusty olive, sea glass
- Accent: burnt sienna, warm white

## Pattern DNA

Wave-influenced forms. Coastal architecture references. Light refractions. Mediterranean mosaic-inspired surfaces. Sun geometry.

## Product types in this collection

- One-piece swimsuits
- Bikinis
- Windbreakers
- Tees
- Shorts
- Dresses
- Backpacks

## Naming convention

`BKS Riviera [Product Type] — [Variant]`

Examples:
- BKS Riviera One-Piece Swimsuit — Coast
- BKS Riviera Windbreaker — Maritime
- BKS Riviera Tee — Lido
- BKS Riviera Dress — Onda

## Target customer

Mediterranean lifestyle, travel-conscious, resort-adjacent, warm-season fashion, 24–45.

## Photography direction

Coastal architecture. Warm light. Terracotta walls. Harbor or pool settings. Golden hour mandatory. Clean natural backgrounds.
