# BKS / BakAbo — Shipping Policy Summary

## Production model

BakAbo products are made on demand. Every item is produced after your order is placed.

This means:
- No pre-built inventory
- Production begins after purchase confirmation
- Total delivery time = production time + shipping time

## Production time

Typical production: **2–7 business days** (varies by product type and print partner availability).

Complex products (puffer jackets, sneakers): may require additional production time.

## Shipping time (estimated by destination)

| Destination | Estimated time |
|-------------|---------------|
| United States | 5–10 business days after production |
| Europe | 5–10 business days after production |
| United Kingdom | 5–10 business days after production |
| Canada | 7–14 business days after production |
| Australia | 7–14 business days after production |
| Rest of world | 10–20 business days after production |

*Times are estimates, not guarantees. Customs clearance may add delays.*

## Shipping carriers

Products are shipped via standard global carriers (varies by country of production and destination).

Tracking information is provided after dispatch.

## International orders

- Customs duties and import taxes may apply depending on destination country
- BakAbo is not responsible for customs fees
- Customers are responsible for import duties at delivery

## Order changes

Once production has started, orders cannot be modified or cancelled. Contact support immediately if there is an error in your order.
