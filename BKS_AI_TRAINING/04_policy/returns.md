# BKS / BakAbo — Returns & Refund Policy Summary

## Made-on-demand nature

Because all BakAbo products are made to order, standard returns for change of mind are generally not accepted. Each product is produced specifically for the customer after purchase.

## When a return or refund is accepted

Returns and replacements are accepted in the following cases:

- **Defective product**: printing defect, damaged item, manufacturing error
- **Wrong item received**: product different from what was ordered
- **Significant quality issue**: clearly below acceptable standard

## What to do if there is a problem

1. Contact BakAbo support within **14 days of delivery**
2. Provide: order number, photos of the issue, description
3. Support will assess and offer: replacement, refund, or store credit depending on the case

## Size returns

Because products are made to order, sizing errors by the customer are not covered as defects.

Check the size guide carefully before ordering.

If there is a defect in sizing accuracy from production, contact support with photos.

## Cancellations

Orders may only be cancelled before production starts.
Contact support immediately if cancellation is needed — production typically begins within 24 hours of order confirmation.

## Refund processing

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

## For up-to-date information

Always refer to: https://bakabo.club/policies/refund-policy

This document is a summary only. Official policy on the store takes precedence.
