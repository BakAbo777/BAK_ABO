# BKS / BakAbo — Warranty & Quality Guarantee Summary

## Product quality guarantee

BakAbo guarantees that products:
- Are printed as designed
- Meet acceptable print quality for all-over print garments
- Are free from manufacturing defects at time of dispatch

## What is covered

- Printing defects (fading, misalignment, unintended blank areas)
- Manufacturing defects (seam failure, structural damage from production)
- Wrong product shipped

## What is not covered

- Normal wear and tear
- Damage caused by improper washing or care
- Customer error in size selection
- Color perception differences between screen and print (all-over print colors may vary slightly from screen representation)

## Care instructions (general)

To maintain print quality:
- Machine wash cold, inside out
- Do not bleach
- Tumble dry low or hang dry
- Do not iron directly on print
- Do not dry clean

Specific care instructions are on the product label.

## Claim process

1. Contact support at https://bakabo.club/pages/contact
2. Provide order number + photos of defect
3. BakAbo will assess and confirm if covered
4. Resolution: replacement or refund depending on case and availability
