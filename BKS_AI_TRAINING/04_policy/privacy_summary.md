# BKS / BakAbo — Privacy Summary

## Data controller

BakAbo / BKS Studio operates the bakabo.club Shopify store.

## Data collected

- Name and email: for order processing and account
- Shipping address: for delivery
- Payment information: processed by Shopify Payments / Stripe — not stored by BakAbo
- Browsing data: Shopify analytics, cookies for store functionality

## Data use

- Order fulfillment and communication
- Customer support
- Optional: marketing emails (only with consent)
- Analytics for store improvement

## Third parties

- **Shopify**: store platform, order data storage
- **Printify**: production partner, receives shipping address for fulfillment
- **Stripe / Shopify Payments**: payment processing
- **Cloudflare**: CDN and worker hosting
- **OpenAI**: AI assistant backend (no personal customer data sent)

## Customer rights

- Access your data: contact support
- Delete your data: request via support
- Unsubscribe from marketing: link in every email

## Cookies

Essential cookies used for cart and session. Analytics cookies only with consent.
