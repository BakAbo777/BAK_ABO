# BKS / BakAbo — Brand Positioning

## Identity

**BakAbo / BKS Studio** is an AI-art fashion atelier designed in Italy and made on demand worldwide.

The brand creates AI-generated all-over print surfaces, curated into editorial collections and applied to garments and accessories produced after each order.

---

## One-line definition

> "Raw editorial artwear. AI-generated BKS surfaces turned into wearable graphic objects."

---

## Positioning formula

BakAbo occupies a precise market position:

| Category | BakAbo |
|----------|--------|
| vs generic POD | More artistic, more editorial, more curated |
| vs fast fashion | Slower, intentional, no overstock |
| vs luxury tailoring | More accessible, more honest, AI-native |
| vs streetwear | More conceptual, more Italian, more visual |
| vs AI experiments | More commercial, more coherent, more finished |

---

## Core values

1. **AI-generated surfaces** — every pattern is created with generative AI
2. **Editorial curation** — patterns are organized into named collections with visual DNA
3. **Designed in Italy** — Roberto Picchioni, BKS Studio, artistic direction
4. **Made on demand** — no overstock, produced after purchase via global print partners
5. **Wearable graphic objects** — garments as carriers of a visual system, not just clothing

## Competitive formula (internal)

BakAbo must always be:
- above generic POD
- more artistic than common streetwear
- more accessible than classic luxury
- more editorial than a standard Printify catalog
- clearer than a confused AI experiment
