# bakabo-photo-studio

Skill for product photography, collection imagery, theme composition and review enablement after Shopify products are created.

## Workflow

1. Detect published/realized Shopify products.
2. Build shot list by product and collection.
3. Produce hero, detail, scale, lifestyle and packaging images.
4. Map images to product media, collection hero, theme bands and social formats.
5. Verify truthfulness: image must show the product reality and not create misleading expectations.
6. Activate review request flow only after delivery/customer experience.
7. Save winning image/theme combinations to Knowledge DB.

## Theme progression

- Trust foundation: trust strip, policy links, product reality, clear image / images: Hero product + detail + scale.
- Collection identity: collection hero, short story, visual rhythm / images: Collection hero + lifestyle context.
- Conversion support: reviews, FAQs, shipping/returns reassurance, related products / images: Detail + packaging + review prompt.
- Campaign layer: timed offer, avatar video, multilingual social proof / images: 9:16 social/video assets + collection hero.

## Collection directions

- Hours: dark raw concrete; hard lateral single source; urban, contemplative.
- Glyph: matte black plane; flat studio ring light; graphic, coded.
- Marker: rough paper / iron; hard lateral with sharp shadow; gestural, urban.
- Riviera: travertine / linen; golden hour from right; resort, mediterranean.
- Pulse: dark plane / tiles; front ring light; optical, kinetic.
- Token: reflective surface / plexiglass; low-key neon accent; arcade, digital.
- Flag: white studio; flat uniform light; pop, graphic.
- Folklore: light stone / linen / earth; soft overcast; narrative, naive.

## Review guardrails

- Honest review only: Ask for real experience; do not script fake reviews.
- Timing: Ask after delivery window or customer confirmation.
- No hidden incentive: If incentive exists, disclose it clearly and follow platform rules.
- Product improvement loop: Negative feedback becomes supplier/product/theme improvement evidence.
