## Shipping Policy

```html
<h1>Shipping Policy</h1>

<p>BKS Studio products are made on demand. Each piece is printed and produced after the order is placed, then shipped through our fulfillment partners.</p>

<h2>Production time</h2>
<p>Most orders require 7-10 business days before shipping. This is production time, not transit time. During peak periods, production can take longer.</p>

<h2>Shipping time</h2>
<p>Shipping times depend on destination, carrier availability, customs processing, and local delivery conditions. Tracking is sent by email when the order ships.</p>

<h2>Split shipments</h2>
<p>Orders with multiple items can ship in separate parcels when products are produced by different fulfillment facilities. You may receive more than one tracking number.</p>

<h2>Customs and import fees</h2>
<p>International orders may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, or local fees. These charges are set by the destination country and are the customer's responsibility unless stated otherwise at checkout.</p>

<h2>Incorrect addresses</h2>
<p>Please check your shipping address before confirming the order. If an address is incomplete or incorrect, delivery may fail or the parcel may be returned to the carrier or fulfillment partner.</p>

<h2>Lost or delayed parcels</h2>
<p>If tracking has not updated for an unusual period, contact us at <a href="mailto:[SUPPORT_EMAIL]">[SUPPORT_EMAIL]</a> with your order number. We will check the order status with the carrier or fulfillment partner.</p>

<h2>Contact</h2>
<p>For shipping questions, contact <a href="mailto:[SUPPORT_EMAIL]">[SUPPORT_EMAIL]</a>.</p>
```

```html

<ul>
```

## About Page

```html
<h1>About BakAbo / BKS Studio</h1>

<p>BakAbo is the container. BKS Studio is the group behind this collection.</p>

<p>BKS Studio creates AI-generated wearable art: prints, patterns, fields, and signs developed through a prompted visual process and curated by hand. The work is produced as garments and accessories through on-demand fulfillment.</p>

<p>The current catalog is built around eight permanent BKS collections: Hours, Glyph, Marker, Riviera, Pulse, Token, Flag, and Folklore. Each collection has its own visual register. Together they form the BKS system.</p>

<p>Nothing is produced before it is ordered. No warehouse, no overstock, no seasonal noise. A piece moves from prompt to pattern, from pattern to product, from product to wardrobe.</p>

<p>Designed in Italy. Produced on demand through selected fulfillment partners.</p>
```

## FAQ Page

```html
<h1>FAQ</h1>

<h2>What is BakAbo?</h2>
<p>BakAbo is the container for BKS Studio projects and collections.</p>

<h2>What is BKS Studio?</h2>
<p>BKS Studio is a digital art studio creating AI-generated wearable art, printed on demand as garments and accessories.</p>

<h2>Are products made before purchase?</h2>
<p>No. Products are made on demand after the order is placed.</p>

<h2>How long does production take?</h2>
<p>Most products require 7-10 business days before shipping. Shipping time starts after production is complete.</p>

<h2>Where are products produced?</h2>
<p>Design and curation are handled by BKS Studio. Production is handled by fulfillment partners, selected according to product type and destination.</p>

<h2>Why can orders ship in separate parcels?</h2>
<p>Different product types can be produced by different fulfillment facilities. This can create separate shipments and tracking numbers.</p>

<h2>Can I return an item?</h2>
<p>If an item is damaged, defective, misprinted, or incorrect, contact us within 14 days of delivery. For other returns, contact us before sending anything back so we can confirm eligibility under the Refund and Returns Policy.</p>

<h2>Are the prints AI-generated?</h2>
<p>Yes. Each print originates from an AI-assisted design process and is curated by BKS Studio before becoming a product.</p>

<h2>Are products limited edition?</h2>
<p>Not unless a product page clearly says so. The current catalog is made on demand, not numbered limited edition.</p>

<h2>How do I contact support?</h2>
<p>Email <a href="mailto:[SUPPORT_EMAIL]">[SUPPORT_EMAIL]</a> with your order number.</p>
```
