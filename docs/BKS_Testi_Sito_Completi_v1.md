  <h1>BKS Studio</h1>
  <p class="bks-hero-line"><em>A digital atelier. Eight collections. AI-generated prints on demand.</em></p>

  <h2>The studio</h2>
  <p>BKS Studio is a design practice built around a single question: what happens when you use AI as a craft tool rather than a shortcut? The answer is a permanent catalog of eight editorial collections — Hours, Glyph, Marker, Riviera, Pulse, Token, Flag, Folklore — each built on a distinct visual system, each generating garments and accessories that could not have existed before.</p>
  <p>The studio operates from Italy. Every print originates from an AI-driven process: a library of art-movement references, curated prompt structures, iterative visual development. The result is not AI for novelty — it is AI as the new instrument of a digital design practice.</p>

  <h2>The process</h2>
  <p>Each BKS collection begins with a reference field: a movement, a visual language, a set of formal rules. Hours draws from hyperrealist urban registers. Glyph builds a proprietary symbolic alphabet. Marker works in gestural, painted marks. The prompts translate these references into print systems — fields, patterns, and compositions tested against fabric, garment silhouette, and scale before a single product is created.</p>
  <p>Every piece is printed after purchase, on demand. No warehouse, no overstock, no clearance. Each order triggers production. The supply chain is the design — leaner, less wasteful, built for a catalog that earns its place.</p>

  <h2>The catalog</h2>
  <p>189 pieces across eight collections. Sneakers, outerwear, swimwear, bags, and more — all carrying the same AI-generated print systems at different scales and on different surfaces. The catalog is permanent: collections do not expire. New products enter through editorial drops, not seasonal cycles.</p>

  <h2>Made to order</h2>
  <p>Every BKS piece is produced after purchase. Production takes 7–14 business days depending on category, after which your order ships. This is not a delay — it is the model. No piece is ever left unsold in a warehouse.</p>

  <h2>Legal</h2>
