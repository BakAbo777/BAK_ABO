# BKS Legal Guardrails

Operational legal/compliance guardrails for BKS Master Agent. This is a checklist and evidence system, not legal advice.

## Customer Guarantees

- Business identity: Customers must understand who sells, how to contact BKS and which domain/brand they are dealing with. Status: needs_fix.
- Consumer information before purchase: Distance contracts need clear pre-contract information, withdrawal procedure, delivery and risk information. Status: pass.
- Guarantees and returns: EU consumers have statutory guarantee rights; returns/refunds must be explained clearly. Status: pass.
- Product safety: Only safe products should be available; product risk, warnings and responsible operators must be traceable. Status: manual_pending.
- Privacy and consent: Personal data and tracking must have a lawful basis; consent must be transparent where needed. Status: pass.
- Email marketing: Commercial email must use truthful sender/subject, clear opt-out and compliant marketing content. Status: pass.
- Payments and crypto: Payment methods must be clear, secure and not framed as investment opportunity. Status: manual_pending.

## Supplier / Producer Relationship Controls

- Printify / production partners: Product specs, print quality, defect handling, fulfillment SLA, returns/claims flow, product safety info. Status: planned_contract.
- Shopify: Platform terms, checkout security, order data processing, theme/app permissions. Status: active_needs_contract_review.
- Payment providers: Settlement, refunds, disputes, chargebacks, crypto handling, prohibited claims. Status: active_needs_contract_review.
- HeyGen / creative AI providers: Usage rights, consent for avatar/voice, commercial license, export retention, takedown path. Status: active_needs_contract_review.
- OpenAI / AI services: Data minimization, no sensitive customer data unless necessary, prompt/output review. Status: active_needs_contract_review.
- Social platforms: API permissions, platform policies, campaign transparency, ad account governance. Status: planned_contract.
- Logistics/shipping partners: Delivery times, tracking, lost parcel process, customer communication handoff. Status: planned_contract.

## Agent rule

The agent may draft customer replies, supplier checklists and compliance evidence. It must request human review for legal, privacy, payment, supplier contract, product safety, complaint and Merchant appeal actions.
