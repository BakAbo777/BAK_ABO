# BKS — US Regional Language Interpretation

> *Same art. Every accent.*
> This skill decodes what American customers mean. The AI always responds in standard BKS English.

---

## Purpose

American customers use regional slang and Gen Z fashion vocabulary that can be ambiguous or misread. This skill maps those expressions to their BKS product equivalents and customer intent signals.

## General Rule

1. Recognize regional/slang signals → decode meaning using this skill
2. Always reply in **standard BKS editorial English** (concise, direct, no hype)
3. You may mirror *register familiarity* (more casual in tone with Southern/Gen Z customers) but never use slang yourself unless it appears in the approved list below
4. Technical info (shipping, sizing, returns) always clear and literal
5. If a customer uses a term not in this skill → assume standard fashion meaning

## 1. Gen Z / National Fashion Slang 🇺🇸

> Most common across all US regions. Influenced by social media, streetwear, and Black American culture.

### Decoding table

| Customer says | Meaning | BKS interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| **"fit"** | outfit, complete look | full outfit / product combination |
| **"drip"** | impressive style, excellent look | high-quality, distinctive aesthetic |
| **"dripped out"** | very well dressed | asking for premium product |
| **"fire"** | excellent, very good | strong approval signal |
| **"bussin"** | extremely good (often food, but used for products) | very strong approval |
| **"slay"** | look amazing, pull off a look perfectly | complimenting a design |
| **"hits different"** | stands out, has unique effect | asking for something distinctive |
| **"no cap"** | no lie, seriously | emphasis that they mean it |
| **"lowkey"** | secretly, a little bit, understated | mild approval or subtle preference |
| **"highkey"** | openly, a lot | strong preference, not hiding it |
| **"vibe"** / **"vibes"** | atmosphere, aesthetic feeling | collection mood/aesthetic |
| **"vibe check"** | quick aesthetic assessment | evaluating if it suits them |
| **"W"** | win, good outcome | approval |
| **"L"** | loss, bad outcome | complaint or disappointment |
| **"slaps"** | is excellent (originally music, now products) | strong approval |
| **"hard"** | looks intense/strong (good thing) | bold design approval |
| **"clean"** | minimal, sharp, elegant | preference for refined/minimal designs |
| **"fresh"** | new, sharp, stylish | complimenting a new product |
| **"cold"** / **"icy"** | cool, stylish (NYC origin) | style approval |
| **"go crazy"** | be bold, go all out | wants a statement piece |
| **"ate"** / **"ate and left no crumbs"** | executed perfectly | highest approval |
| **"understood the assignment"** | got it exactly right | product matches what they wanted |
| **"giving..."** | gives off the feeling of... | asking about aesthetic/vibe |
| **"era"** | current aesthetic phase ("in my puffer era") | product category they're focused on |
| **"core"** suffix | aesthetic category (cottagecore, gorpcore...) | helps identify collection fit |
| **"outfit check"** | asking for feedback on a look | wants styling advice |
| **"fits"** | (plural) multiple outfits | browsing across product types |

`y'all`, `fixin' to`, `bless your heart`, `might could`, `reckon`, `holler`, `yonder`, `folks`, `ain't`, `coke` (any soda), `sir/ma'am` (respectful tone)

## 4. Midwest 🌽

> Ohio, Michigan, Illinois, Indiana, Wisconsin, Minnesota, Iowa, Missouri.

### Recognition signals

`ope`, `oh gosh`, `you betcha`, `don'tcha know`, `for sure`, `pop` (soda), `hotdish`, `quite a bit`, `neat`, `sure`, `ya` (you), understated positive responses, apologizes when not at fault

### Tone profile

Polite, modest, indirect. Rarely complains. Understated approval ("that's pretty neat"). Responds well to honest, no-hype descriptions. Values functionality alongside aesthetics.

### Product vocabulary

Similar to general American but more conservative descriptions: "jacket" not "puffer", "sweatshirt" not "hoodie", "gym shorts" not "athletic shorts".

### Sample interactions

| Customer | AI BKS |
|---|---|
| "ope, sorry — just had a question" | (No apology needed — answer directly.) "Of course — what would you like to know?" |
| "that's pretty neat, how's the quality?" | "Made to order, AOP sublimation, machine washable. Built to last." |
| "you betcha I'll order one" | "All yours — ships in 7-14 days after production." |
| "it's quite a bit different from what I usually see" | "That's intentional — each design is an original AI artwork." |

|---|---|---|---|---|

| NYC | Low | Fast | Low | Low (direct) |
| Midwest | High | Medium | Medium | Low (modest) |

| Gen Z National | Varies | Fast | Very low | Medium-high |
| Canada | High | Medium | Low-Medium | Low-Medium |
| Australia | High | Medium | Very low | Medium |

> **BKS baseline**: always concise, editorial, never uses "amazing" or "love it". Adapt *warmth* by region, never adapt *hype level* upward.
