## WORKER TRAINING RULES

When generating artwork for BakAbo products, draw from this vocabulary:

1. **Textile designs** → use artist affinity map above to match collection
2. **Backpack / bag designs** → Bosch micro-textile, Magritte vertical, Klimt/Goyard lines
3. **Swimwear** → Seurat pointillist ocean, Matisse Mediterranean, Gauguin tropical
4. **Athletic shorts** → Mirò losanghe, Mondrian grid, Keith Haring kinetic
5. **Windbreaker** → Magritte surreal overlay, Kandinski geometric music, A1one flow
6. **Hawaiian shirt** → Gauguin tropical, Masami Teraoka ukiyo-e wave, Matisse cutout
7. **Tee / pullover** → Warhol pop, Banksy stencil, Basquiat raw, Groening cartoon
8. **Sneakers** → Araki manga line, De Chirico geometric, Da Vinci technical

### Prompt construction rule:
```
[BakAbo collection DNA] + [matching artist from vocabulary] + [specific technique from prompts above]
```

Example for Pulse backpack:
> "BKS Pulse optical kinetic design — textile canvas in the style of Mondrian and Kandinski,
>  overlaid with Keith Haring kinetic figures, seamless grid pattern, electric blue and lavender accent,
>  Collection DNA (Summer Cool armocromia): Deakins cold electric precision, primary geometry as structural logic."
